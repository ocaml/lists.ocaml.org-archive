OCaml.org has multi-lingual support. See for example the Mailing Lists page
[1], which in the top-right corner lists the languages that page is
available in. The main problem is volunteers willing to do the
translations. Most pages are not translated and those that are fall behind
when the master English pages are updated. There are plans to facilitate
this [2], but the main need is volunteers.

We recently had offers for Chinese [3] and Russian [4] translations. Please
contribute!
