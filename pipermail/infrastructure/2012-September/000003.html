Dear ocamlweb-devel list,

Here are some quick updates:

* I gave a short demo of the site at OUD, which was well received.

* Xavier Leroy has agreed to allow hosting it on the ocaml.org domain. This
is very good news and makes the site more official. We are working to
import content from the current Inria site (much is already done, and there
are open Issues marking each item remaining).

* The ocaml repo has been moved to https://github.com/ocaml/ocaml.org. This
makes it more official and less tied to any particular individual.

* We are working on a permanent hosting solution. Once setup, we'll be able
to point ocaml.org to this new server.

As always, your contributions are welcome, and now you can be confident
that your work will benefit the community by going directly onto ocaml.org.

Best,
Ashish
