The general way of calling blocking things from inside async is to use
the In_thread module. In particular, here you should be able to do
something like:

  In_thread.run (fun () ->
    Core.Unix.create_process ~prog:"/usr/bin/ssh" ~args:["my-host";
"ls"; "/tmp"])
